Operational Recommendations:
   1. Prioritize early identification of high-acuity elderly
      patients (Cluster with >60% admission rate) for fast-track
      bed allocation to reduce ED boarding times.
   2. Implement care coordination programs for frequent-flyer
      patients (>10 prior ED visits) - small population but
      disproportionate resource consumption.
   3. Use pressure_index (combining ESI, acuity, prior visits/
      admissions) as an early-warning score at triage.
   4. Cardiovascular medication history is the strongest single
      predictor - consider flagging these patients for closer
      monitoring at triage.
